# Visory 3-Month SEO & Conversion Strategy - Executive Summary

## **Current State Assessment**
Website suffers from outdated content (2021/2022), keyword stuffing, unclear value proposition, weak social proof, missing technical SEO elements, and poor conversion optimization. Competitive analysis shows local competitors with stronger online presence and better reviews.

---

## **Month 1: Foundation & Trust Building**
*Goal: Fix critical trust and credibility issues that prevent conversions*

### Week 1-2: Immediate Trust Signals
- Google Business Profile setup for other office locations and reviews examined
- Testimonials overhaul with video and specific results
- Display addresses, certifications and credentials prominently

### Week 3-4: Service Clarity & Benefits
- Homepage rewrite focusing on clear value proposition for different business offerings
- Benefits-focused service page redesign
- "How It Works" page creation with visual/video guides
- Industry-specific landing pages with free guide for that industry

### Week 3-4: Technical Quick Wins
- Schema markup review & implementation
- Xero partner directory links
- Image sizes, Wordpress plugin clearout

---

## **Month 2: Content & Conversion Optimization**
*Goal: Improve organic visibility and convert more visitors*

### Week 5-6: Conversion Rate Optimization
- Interactive elements (ROI calculator, free trial/demo)
- Chatbot implementation, no need to organise a call,  instant feedback
- Pricing transparency with clear tier structure and time/benefit
- Video content creation (hero, how-it-works, integrations, founder story)

### Week 7-8: Content Strategy & Keyword Targeting
- Comprehensive keyword research using GSC etc
- Content mapping and gap analysis
- Blog content should be unique and visory expertise heavy
- Top 5 pages rewritten without keyword stuffing explaining benefits in time/money benefits

---

## **Month 3: Authority Building & Scale**
*Goal: Build topical authority and expand reach*

### Week 9-10: Content Expansion
- Continued blog publishing based on successfule customer implementations
- Resource hub creation with downloadable lead magnets, Xero tips and tricks
- Detailed case studies development (3-5 clients/verticals)

### Week 11-12: Off-Page SEO & Local Presence
- Local SEO optimization and directory submissions
- Partner/Expert section redesign


---

## **Ongoing: Measurement & Iteration**

### Week 1-12: Technical SEO Audit
- Core Web Vitals optimization
- Mobile optimization refinement
- Complete technical and plugin review and refinement

### Tools Setup 
Google Search Console, Analytics 4, Tag Manager, Hotjar/Clarity, Business Profile

### Cadence
- Weekly: Monitor rankings, respond to reviews, analyze user behavior
- Monthly: Review traffic, rankings, conversions, and content performance

---
